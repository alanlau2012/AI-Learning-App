## Style Prompt

A calm engineering learning-book explainer for an enterprise AI education product. The video should feel like a precise interactive textbook made for senior engineers and AI platform leads: warm paper, restrained blue emphasis, readable mechanism diagrams, measured motion, and no marketing gloss.

## Colors

- Background paper: `#FAF9F6`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft surface: `#F2EFE7`
- Border: `#E7E3D9`
- Title: `#1A1916`
- Body text: `#34322C`
- Muted text: `#8C887E`
- Primary blue: `#1F40D8`
- Primary soft: `#EBEEFE`
- Progress green: `#2E7D58`
- Warning amber: `#E8943A`

## Typography

- Headlines: `"IBM Plex Serif", Georgia, "Times New Roman", serif`
- Body/UI: `"IBM Plex Sans", "Segoe UI", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Data labels and tokens: `"IBM Plex Mono", Consolas, monospace`

## Motion

- Medium-energy explainer pacing.
- Primary transition: horizontal push slide.
- Topic transition: staggered full-frame blocks.
- Closing transition: blur crossfade.
- Entrances use varied direction, scale, and opacity; first motion starts after 0.1s.

## What NOT to Do

- No Huawei red primary visual.
- No large dark cyberpunk canvas.
- No neon glow, purple-blue gradients, or dashboard density.
- No heavy shadow card stacks.
- No oversized landing-page hero treatment.
